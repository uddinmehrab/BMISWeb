Help Build Our New Masjid

Alhamdulillah, we have been blessed with a unique opportunity to establish a masjid at the heart of Burnaby, British Columbia. BMIS has acquired a strategically located property that will serve as a central Islamic center for the growing Muslim community in Greater Vancouver. Your contribution to this noble cause, whether small or large, is an investment in the House of Allah that will continue to reward you even after this life.

The Prophet Muhammad ﷺ said: "Whoever builds a mosque for Allah, Allah will build for him a similar house in Paradise." (Sahih Bukhari)

By supporting this project, you're not just helping to build a structure, but establishing a center for worship, education, and community service that will benefit generations to come. Please choose from the donation options below to contribute to this blessed endeavor.
