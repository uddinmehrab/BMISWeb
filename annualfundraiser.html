Our mosque has always been more than a place of worship; it is the heart of our community—a space where we gather for prayer, education, and support. To continue serving our growing community and providing the programs and services we rely on, we need your help.

We warmly invite you to join us for a special Fundraising Dinner, where your participation will make a lasting impact. This event is an opportunity for us to come together, enjoy a delightful evening, and contribute to the future of our mosque.

Your generosity helps us:

  • Maintain and enhance our mosque facilities, including creating plans to buy our own property.
  • Support ongoing educational programs for children and adults.
  • Expand initiatives that serve the community, including outreach and charitable efforts.
  • Ensure that our mosque remains a welcoming and vibrant space for everyone.
