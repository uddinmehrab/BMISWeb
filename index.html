Our Services

Quran Classes

Our Quran classes are designed to cater to all ages and levels of understanding. Whether you are a beginner learning to recite the Quran with proper Tajweed or someone seeking to deepen your knowledge through Tafseer (interpretation), we have tailored sessions to suit your needs. Classes are conducted in a supportive and engaging environment, encouraging students to connect with the Quran's teachings and implement them in their daily lives.

Prayer Facilities

Baitul Mukarram Islamic Society offers a well-maintained and clean facility for the five daily prayers, as well as Jumu'ah (Friday) prayers. The mosque includes spacious prayer areas and dedicated spaces for men and women. Additionally, there are clean and accessible Wudu (ablution) facilities to ensure comfort and convenience for all Muslims.

Community Outreach

We take pride in serving the community through various outreach programs aimed at uplifting and supporting those in need. Our initiatives include food distribution drives, educational workshops, and assistance for underprivileged families. We also organize blood donation campaigns and collaborate with local organizations to provide resources and aid during emergencies.

About Us

Baitul Mukarram Islamic Society (BMIS) is a non-profit registered Muslim charitable organization that provides a range of social services to the local community in British Columbia. Aside from prayer and outreach services, we offer voluntary teaching and learning of basic Islamic education, including recitation of the Holy Quran for all ages with Tajweed and understanding its meaning.

Alhamdulillah, we have begun our journey to establish a central masjid in the heart of Burnaby to serve the growing Muslim community in the Greater Lower Mainland of BC. With the acquisition of a strategically located property, we are working toward creating a comprehensive Islamic center that will be a beacon of faith and community service. Our aim is to build a strong community based on Quranic teachings and the prophetic Sunnah, upholding Islamic values and providing essential services to help Muslims succeed in this life and the hereafter.
