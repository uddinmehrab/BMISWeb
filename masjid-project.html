Project Overview

Baitul Mukarram Islamic Society (BMIS) has acquired a new property to establish one of the largest Masjids in North America. This ambitious project aims to create a comprehensive Islamic center that will serve as a beacon of faith and community service.

Project Goals

  • Establish a large Masjid with comprehensive facilities
  • Provide funeral services for the deceased
  • Create separate full-time madrasahs for boys and girls
  • Offer afternoon Islamic schools for all grades
  • Implement full-time and part-time Tahfiz programs for all ages
  • Develop orphanage care and other religious outreach programs
  • Organize social and community service initiatives
